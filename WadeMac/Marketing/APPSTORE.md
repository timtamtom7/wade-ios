# WadeMac — App Store Listing

## Tagline
**"Your AI-powered travel companion."**

## Subtitle
Plan trips, split expenses, and travel smarter — all in one beautiful app.

## Description
Wade is the ultimate travel companion for iOS and macOS. Plan your trips with AI-powered suggestions, split expenses effortlessly with friends, keep track of packing lists, and stay organized from departure to return.

**Features:**
- **AI Trip Planning** — Get intelligent destination suggestions and itinerary recommendations
- **Expense Splitting** — Track and split travel costs with friends and travel groups
- **Real-time Flight Tracking** — Monitor flight status and get delay alerts
- **Collaborative Planning** — Vote on destinations, activities, and accommodations with your travel group
- **Packing Lists** — Smart packing suggestions and shared checklists
- **Currency Conversion** — Live exchange rates and tip calculator

Wade works offline with your data stored securely on device.

### Design Guidelines
- Use real, contextual content (actual trip names, places, realistic amounts)
- Show the app's best features prominently
- Use the ocean blue (`#0077B6`) and sunset orange (`#FF6B35`) brand colors in screenshots
- Dark mode screenshots preferred for professional feel
- Include app icon in corner (optional, can be omitted)
